Peace Corps and Africa

Challenge

The African Studies Program at University of Wisconsin -Madison organized a conference honoring 50 years of the Peace Corps in Africa in 2011. The program hoped to have a conference website to provide all participants the up-to-date conference agenda and logistics information, and to document all event-related materials for archiving purpose.

Solution

I designed and developed the Peace Corps and Africa Conference website, while working as a website assistant at the African Studies Program.
