When Mondrian Meets Pong

A two-player version of the classic pong game, inspired by Piet Mondrian's painting Composition with Red Blue and Yellow.

press "W": left paddle go up;
press "S": left paddle go down;
press "O": right paddle go up;
press "L": right paddle go up;
