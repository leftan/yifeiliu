ReSAKSS Conference

Challenge

Partnered with the African Union, the International Food Policy Research Institute hosted a high-level conference on “Promoting Agricultural Trade to Enhance Resilience in Africa” in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia in 2014. There were about 250+ participants coming from different countries, and more than 30 participants could only speak French. The purpose of designing a conference website was to provide all participants the most recent conference agenda, logistics, and side event information online, and to document all event-related materials for archive.

Solution

I designed and developed a mobile-friendly and bilingual conference website. I used Bootstrap to make it fully responsible on mobiles and tablets. I designed the conference logo using Adobe Illustrator. The yin-yang shape of the logo represented both production and consumer sides of the agricultural value chain.
