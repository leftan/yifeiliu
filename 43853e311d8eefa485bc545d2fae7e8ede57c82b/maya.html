Sanctuary

I took a 3D Animation class with the UC Berkeley Undergraduate Graphics Group (UCBUGG) in Fall 2017, and my team made a short movie about two dinosaurs. All production work were made in Autodesk Maya, and post production compositing was done in Adobe After Effects.
