Mechanization in Asia & Africa

Challenge

The International Food Policy Research Institute and The National School of Development at Peking University organized a workshop on agricultural mechanization in Asia and Africa in Beijing on June 2014. The goal of the workshop was to facilitate south-south knowledge exchange among researchers, policymakers, and private sector participants for lessons and experiences of mechanization and agricultural transformation in Asian and African countries.

Solution

I was appointed to design an event agenda for the mechanization workshop. I used Adobe InDesign to layout the agenda brochure, and I used Adobe Illustrator to design the workshop logo.
