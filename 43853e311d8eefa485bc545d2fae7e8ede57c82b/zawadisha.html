Infographic & Illustration

Solar Lamp Initiatives

Challenge

Zawadisha is a social enterprise whose mission is to provide small loans to rural Kenyan women to help finance their livelihoods. In 2014, the Zawadisha team hoped to create some infographics to increase the visibility of its sustainable microlending project, share with donors, and raise more money for the project.

Solution

I was hired by Zawadisha team to design the infographics about its solar lighting initiatives and rainwater collection systems. I used Adobe Illustrator to design icons and graphs pertinent to African people.
