Big Data for Africa

Challenge

Founded in Nigeria in 2014, Big Data for Africa provides data services that complements traditional practices, and enhances the overall understanding of citizen welfare in Africa. The startup company needed a branding identity to communicate its core value and vision.

Solution

I was hired by the Big Data for Africa team to design their logo. I chose orange and black as the color palette to represent a bold and vibrant startup image. The combined light bulb shape and map of Africa reflects the core value of the company to use evidence-based information to empower the continent.
