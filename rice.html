Growing Rice Smartly in Asia

Challenge

Given the changing Asian economy and the importance of rice in the region, the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) developed a new regional rice strategy to in 2014 to facilitate agricultural transformation and ensure food security in Asian countries.

Solution

Working as a communication specialist at the International Food Policy Research Institute, I designed an infographic to summarize and share the building blocks of FAO’s new rice strategy in Asia with NGOs, government officials, researchers and policymakers. I used Adobe Illustrator to design the infographic. The art work was featured on the agriculture blog of the Feed The Future Project, U.S Government’s global hunger and food security initiative led by the United States Agency for International Development.
