AirPortal

Challenge

Changi Airport would like to bring innovative ideas and use new and emerging technologies to bring these ideas to the future of the Changi Airport. With over 150,000 passengers going through Changi Airport everyday, the airport faces the challenge to match visitors with the right activities and providing them with the right information at the right time.

Solution
