  2. Client: GPS Leadership Solutions

GPS Leadership Solutions

Challenge

GPS (Goals, Plans, and Strategies) Leadership Solutions provides hands-on trainings and workshops for young professionals to develop leadership skills and career strategies. In 2013, the startup company would like to design a new branding identity to communicate its core value and vision with clients.

Solution

I was hired by GPS Leadership Solutions team to design their logo. Four arrows represent different career paths that young professionals may develop. The arrow pointing to the right reflects the company’s vision, which is to help young people to develop the “right” career based on their interests and needs.
